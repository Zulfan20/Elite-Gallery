News about Elite Gallery

"Brush & Pallets" Workshop Raises Environmental Awareness

The "Brush & Pallets" workshop was successfully held on Sunday, March 31st at LT2. Running from 2:30 PM to 6:30 PM, the event attracted many participants. The workshop aimed to raise awareness about environmental conservation through plastic upcycling. In addition to gaining new knowledge, attendees enjoyed provided snacks and iftar. The event received high praise from participants, who felt more motivated to contribute positively to the environment.

T-shirt Art Workshop Successfully Held at Elite Gallery

On 28 May 2024, Elite Gallery successfully held a T-shirt Art Workshop attended by 10 enthusiastic participants. The event, which ran from 8pm to 10pm, provided a hands-on experience in designing t-shirts under the guidance of experienced instructors. With a registration fee of RM 15, participants received all the necessary materials and took home their work. The session not only honed art skills but also strengthened the creative community among AIU students. Participants thoroughly enjoyed the collaborative and inspiring atmosphere, producing a variety of unique designs that reflected their personalities. Elite Gallery would like to thank everyone who contributed to the success of this event.

Canting Batik Workshop Successfully Held at Albukhary International School

On May 31, 2024, Elite Gallery, in collaboration with Albukhary International School, successfully held a Canting Batik Workshop for children under 12 years old, from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM. With a registration fee of RM 50, participants received painting tools, canvases, as well as food and beverages. Under the guidance of experienced instructors, the children enthusiastically learned and practiced the art of batik, creating their own masterpieces. The event was filled with cheerful and vibrant energy, with each participant taking home their creation along with a special gift. Parents appreciated this opportunity to introduce their children to cultural heritage. Elite Gallery and Albukhary International School thank everyone who supported the event, hoping to instill a love for art and culture from an early age.

Elite Gallery Successfully Participates in AIU Night Market

Elite Gallery successfully participated in the AIU Night Market on 5 June 2024 at the Chancellery Building Corridor, Albukhary International University (AIU). They displayed a variety of handcrafted products, such as customised tote bags, customised t-shirts, and paintings at affordable prices. Elite Gallery's booth attracted a lot of attention from visitors, introducing their unique products to AIU students.
